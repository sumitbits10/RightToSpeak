Fighting for Legal Awareness Across India

Advocate Akant Goswami is a practising lawyer based in Dhanbad, Jharkhand with a passion for making the Indian legal system understandable for every citizen — regardless of their education or economic background.

Growing up in Jharkhand, Akant witnessed how lack of legal awareness often left ordinary people vulnerable — unable to file a complaint, unaware of their rights, or easily exploited by those who knew the system. This experience became the driving force behind Right To Speak.

Mission

"Every Indian citizen deserves to know their rights. The law is written for everyone — but it only works if people understand it. Right To Speak exists to bridge that gap, one video at a time."

— Adv. Akant Goswami

Why Right To Speak?

Most legal content in India is either too technical, available only in English, or hidden behind paywalls. Akant creates videos in simple Hindi, covering real-life situations that affect common people — what to do if police refuse to file your FIR, how to approach consumer court, what rights you have as a tenant.

The platform has grown entirely organically through YouTube and Instagram, reaching thousands of viewers across Jharkhand and beyond.
