Aaj ke zamane mein online shopping aur local markets mein cheating aam baat ho gayi hai. Lekin bahut kam log jaante hain ki unke paas ek bahut bada hathiyaar hai — Consumer Protection Act 2019.



\## Consumer Court Kya Hota Hai?



Consumer Court ek special court hai jo sirf consumers yaani grahak ki problems sunte hain. Yahan aap bina vakeel ke bhi apna case lad sakte hain. Filing fee bhi bahut kam hoti hai.



\## Kaun Complaint Kar Sakta Hai?



\- Jisne koi product kharida ho aur wo kharab nikla

\- Jise koi service di gayi ho lekin properly nahi di gayi

\- Jisse koi company ne extra paise liye hon

\- Jisse koi false advertisement se nuksaan hua ho



\## Kahan File Karein?



Court ka choice depend karta hai aapke claim ki raqam par:



\- \*\*District Commission\*\* — 50 lakh rupaye tak ke claims ke liye

\- \*\*State Commission\*\* — 50 lakh se 2 crore tak

\- \*\*National Commission\*\* — 2 crore se upar



\## Step by Step Process



\### Step 1 — Complaint likhein

Ek saada kagaz par likhen ki kya hua, kab hua, aur aapko kitna nuksaan hua. Saath mein apna naam, address aur company ka naam likhein.



\### Step 2 — Documents taiyaar karein

Yeh saare documents saath rakhein:

\- Receipt ya bill

\- Product ki photo (agar kharab hai)

\- Company se jo bhi messages ya emails hue hain

\- Warranty card (agar ho)



\### Step 3 — Complaint file karein

Aap do tarike se complaint file kar sakte hain:



\*\*Online:\*\* edaakhil.gov.in par jaayein — yeh government ka free portal hai Consumer Court complaints ke liye.



\*\*Offline:\*\* Apne District Consumer Commission office mein jakar 3 copies jama karein.



\### Step 4 — Fee bharein

Claim ki raqam ke hisaab se nominal fee hoti hai. 5 lakh tak ke claims ke liye sirf 200 rupaye.



\### Step 5 — Sunwai ka intezaar karein

Notice company ko jaata hai. Dono sides apni baat rakhte hain. Decision aata hai.



\## Important Baat



Complaint karne ki time limit 2 saal hoti hai jab se problem aayi ho. Isliye der mat karein.



Agar aapko Dhanbad ya Jharkhand mein Consumer Court complaint mein madad chahiye, toh hume seedha contact karein.
